About the Engineer.

My journey into Artificial Intelligence didn't start with algorithms; it started with a frustration for inefficient systems. As a self-taught developer initially focused on web interfaces, I quickly realized that the true bottleneck wasn't how things looked, but how they reasoned. This realization drove me to dive deep into machine learning, transitioning from building static UIs to developing intelligent, adaptive systems.

Today, I operate at the intersection of full-stack engineering and applied AI. I specialize in taking complex, computationally heavy models and wrapping them in elegant, highly performant interfaces. My focus isn't just on training models in a vacuum, but on deploying them into production environments where they can solve real-world problems.

I believe the next generation of software won't just be 'used'; it will be conversed with. My current research and personal projects are heavily focused on leveraging Large Language Models (LLMs) to create generative applications that augment human creativity rather than replace it.
